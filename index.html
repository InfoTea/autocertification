Product Overview

AutoCertification is a fully customizable document automation platform for laboratories and certification centers. One data entry instantly generates the entire package of documents — orders, protocols, reports, and certificates. The system adapts to any business process: templates are tuned to your local documentation standards, and all data — clients, vehicles, and test results — are stored in a unified database. In Kazakhstan, AutoCertification is already used for preparing and managing documents under the SBKTS (Certificate of Vehicle Safety) framework, ensuring compliance with national technical regulations. The platform can be customized for other types of certification and testing workflows as well.
  • Customizable templates. We configure document templates to match your exact forms and requirements.
  • Unified database. All records — orders, test results, certificates — are stored together and linked by fields you choose.
  • Automatic journal generation. Build and export reports or registers by any field — instantly.
  • Continuous technical support. Our team provides 7 days a week assistance, from 9 AM to 11 PM, updates, and maintenance.
  • Data ownership and transparency: all information is stored on the customer's own Google Drive, ensuring full access to entered data even if cooperation ends.

AutoCertification can be deployed for various industries — from automotive certification to laboratory testing — with flexible data structures and pricing.
